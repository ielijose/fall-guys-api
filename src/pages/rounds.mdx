### Get all rounds

You can access the list of rounds by using the `/rounds` endpoint.

```json
https://fallguysapi.com/api/rounds/
```

### Get a single round

You can get a single round by adding the `id` as a parameter: `/rounds/5f5231ec2a7a2d59f9a2386f`

```json
https://fallguysapi.com/api/rounds/5f5231ec2a7a2d59f9a2386f
```
